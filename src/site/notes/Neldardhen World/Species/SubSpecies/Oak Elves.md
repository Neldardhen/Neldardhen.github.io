# Oak Elves
A Subspecie of [[Neldardhen World/Species/Elves\|Elves]].

They are more sturdy than their cousins, but also less agile or flexible

> [!sys] System Details
>
> **Slow** :  You loose **2m** of walking speed.
>
> **Tough** : You gain **+1** to your Body score.
