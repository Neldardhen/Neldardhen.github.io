Neldar

Character Progression

Progression for the player characters in the Neldar System.

The Game Master award new level when a significant milestone of the story has been reached.
Every time a character gains a level they also gain :

  • 2 points to spend in theri Fundamental Values (see Basics)
  • 10 points to buy new attribute dice or skill ranks (see Basics - Skills)
  • 1 Feat

Characters gains a Skill Feat at level 2 and every 3 levels after that (except at level 20).
(2, 5, 8, 11, 14, 17)

Characters gains a Species Feat at level 3 and every 3 levels after that.
(3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18)

Characters gains a General Feat at level 4 and every 3 levels after that. (except at level 10)
(4, 7, 13, 16, 19)

Characters gains a Power Feat at level 10 and 20.

To get a Feat a Character must meet all the prerequisite listed on the feat. If they somehow stop to meet those prerequisites they loose the feat. (When a feat is lost a new one can be chosen in its place.)
