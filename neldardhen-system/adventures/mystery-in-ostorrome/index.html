Mystery IN Ostorrómë

#Adventure/Mystery
Short adventure taking place in Ostorrómë. (Use the very first draft of the Neldar System, it is not up to date anymore.)

List of Locations, NPCs and Handouts

Adventure start 16 of Dandoledniss

Hook

You will all be in the city of Ostorrómë in Talarún. You may know each others or not, that's up to you, but you all know Daîrgon the old goblin gardener of the University. Daîrgon has asked for your help with something, which is rather unusual because he is not the kind of person to ask help from others. You are to meet him in the gardens just before the sunset, the time at which he finishes his work.

Character Creation

Starting level

For this adventure you will create a level 1 character.

Free Competence Points

You character has been living in or has being visiting Ostorrómë frequently enough to be able to speak the local language. You get a free 30 points in East Palanian or Talanian, if you already chose one of those language as you native one, you can spend the 30 points in any other language of your choice.

Currency

You will start the game with :
(1d2) Iris
(2d4) Petals
(2d20) Seeds

Summary of what happenned

Nolmo a scholar of The Other Truth and teacher at the University of Ostorrómë is trying to open a portal to the "Earth Moon", he instead keep opening unstable portals to Nyalë - the Dusk World.

Various kind of incidents happened depending on where the portals have been open and where they were leading.

Incident #1 : Flash Flood

When : Sometime in the previous month, late evening. (17 of Ringpeleth)

Where : In a "lab", on the second floor of the University, next to the library.

What : Nolmo and his apprentice (Istyar) open a portal to Nyalë directly into a river. It stayed open just long enough to flood the room and the library next door.

Incident #2 : The creatures

When : Around 2 weeks ago, late evening (2 of Dandoledniss)

Where : In the University Gardens

What : Nolmo opened a second portal to Nyalë. Two Lynyals wandered into it, scaring Nomla and Istyar away. The portal re-opens weakly every time the moon rises and re-closes when the moon sets again.

The Lynyals are stressed and exhausted from living in a world they are not used to. (see University Gardens)

Incident #3 : Destroyed house

When : The evening the adventure start (16 of Dandoledniss)

Where : In Nolmo's office, in his home.

What : Nolmo tried to open a portal alone in his office. It failed and destroyed part of his house.

Incident #4 : Future attempt

When : In five days, midnight (21 of Dandoledniss) coincide with the start of Shassleever

Where : At Istyar's home

What : Nolmo ans Istyar will try again to open a portal to Nyalë. This time they will succeed, but three Kelagars will come through and kill them.
